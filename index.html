About Himu Softech India

Himu Softech India Pvt Ltd was founded in 2013 by Lokesh Ramdas, a Computer Science engineer, with a vision to deliver practical, reliable, and affordable software solutions to SMEs, distributors, and community organisations. Over the years, the company has grown into a trusted technology partner for LPG distributors, IOCL projects, educational institutions, trusts, and MSMEs.

We specialise in Google Sheets automations, data dashboards, full‑stack web applications, and cloud‑based MSME software. Our focus is on building tools that are simple, accurate, and deliver clear ROI — from distributor daily ops platforms to grievance management and accounting automation.

Our Journey

2013

Company founded by Lokesh Ramdas, Computer Science engineer, with focus on SME software.

2014

Launch of MyTracker, a vehicle tracking system for all IOCL bottling plants in Karnataka.

2015

Early automation projects in billing, stock, and accounting workflows.

2016

Launch of LPG Suvidha, a delivery application for IOCL distributors, integrated with a payment gateway.

2018

Development of Geo RTD (Round Trip Distance) for IOCL bottling plants in Karnataka to calculate shortest routes for LPG truck deliveries.

2019–2022

Expansion into dashboards and PWAs for SMEs, trusts, and community organisations, enabling remote operations during the pandemic.

2023

Rollout of Cloud Accounting & GST Automation with SDMS→Tally integration for zero‑error compliance.

2024

Introduction of MSME‑focused solutions: multilingual UI systems, grievance management platforms, and secure cloud deployments.

2025

Launch of the new LPG Suvidha (Daily Operations Platform), a comprehensive operational hub for LPG distributors covering delivery updates, godown sales, stock receipts, and multilingual live dashboards.
